#GLOSSARY
#Pre exponent values are set in log values.
R - ideal gas constant = 8.3144621 j/(mol K)
T - has to be in Kelvin
P - has to be in GPa
pat_fact - Correction factor for studies using calibration of Paterson (1982)

------------model parameters------------------------------

sigma_i - Ionic conduction log-pre-exponent (S/m)
sigma_i_err - Ionic conduction log-pre-exponent error (S/m)
h_i - Ionic conduction activation enthalpy (J/mol)
h_i_err - Ionic conduction activation enthalpy error (J/mol)

sigma_pol - Polaron conduction log-pre-exponent (S/m)
sigma_pol - Polaron conduction log-pre-exponent error (S/m)
h_pol - Polaron conduction activation enthalpy (J/mol)
h_pol_err - Polaron conduction activation enthalpy error (J/mol)
alpha_pol - Polaron conduction iron content coefficient (J/mol)
alpha_pol_err - Polaron conduction iron content coefficient error (J/mol)

sigma_p - Proton conduction log-pre-exponent (S/m)
sigma_p_err - Proton conduction log-pre-exponent error (S/m)
r - coefficient for controlling water species (unitless)
r_err - coefficient for controlling water species error (unitless)
h_p - Proton conduction activaiton enthalpy (J/mol)
h_p_err - Proton conduction activaiton enthalpy error (J/mol) 
alpha_p - Proton conduction water content coefficient (J/mol)
alpha_p_err - Proton conduction water content coefficient error (J/mol)
